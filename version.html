v1.0
Version 1.0
The inaugural release of our Multilingual Platform marks the beginning of our journey to break language barriers in digital tools. This foundational version establishes our core infrastructure and delivers our first fully functional tool to serve diverse linguistic communities across India.

Key Features

  • Multilingual Calculator: Our flagship tool provides advanced calculation capabilities with seamless number-to-word translation across six Indian languages - English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam. The calculator features a beautiful, responsive interface that makes complex calculations intuitive and accessible. Perfect for education, business, and daily use, it bridges the gap between numerical literacy and linguistic preference, ensuring everyone can work with numbers in their mother tongue.
  • Complete Platform Infrastructure: Built with modern web technologies featuring responsive design, smooth animations, and intuitive navigation
  • Six Language Support: Full multilingual interface supporting English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam
  • Privacy-First Design: All calculations and operations happen locally in the browser with no data collection
  • Mobile-Responsive: Seamless experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
